About Us

A two-person digital agency combining web development, marketing and data.

We combine creative execution, technical expertise and analytical thinking to deliver websites, digital marketing strategies, market research and data-driven business solutions.

Aissam Boudrouz

Data Science & Marketing Analytics Specialist

Aissam Boudrouz specializes in data science, marketing analytics, market research and business strategy. He holds a Master’s degree in Data Science from Hassan II University in Morocco and an MBA in Project Management from the Université du Québec en Outaouais.

He also completed training in Digital Marketing through the University of Barcelona, strengthening his expertise in digital strategy, online customer acquisition, social media marketing, e-commerce and marketing performance analysis.

Within the agency, Aissam leads the data science, marketing analytics, market research and strategic analysis side. His work focuses on customer behavior analysis, sales forecasting, dashboards, campaign performance, strategic recommendations and data-driven business decisions.

Ismaeil Ben Hamad

Web Development & Digital Solutions Specialist

Ismaeil holds a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the Université du Québec en Outaouais, with a specialization in Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence. He has experience in web development, digital marketing, community management and digital business support.

Within the agency, Ismaeil leads the website development and digital solutions side. He focuses on creating modern, responsive and user-friendly websites for restaurants, cafés, entrepreneurs and small businesses.

His work includes professional websites, digital menus, landing pages, branding direction, online visibility solutions and social media support. By combining technical expertise, creativity and business understanding, he helps clients build a strong and professional online presence.
